Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

It's a focused 30-minute conversation where I ask sharp questions about your business, the problem you're experiencing, and the metric you need to move. By the end, I'll have a clear enough picture to tell you: (a) whether what you're describing is something I can genuinely help with, (b) what architecture I'd recommend, and (c) a rough timeline and engagement model. No pitch deck. No pressure. Just a clear answer.

Yes. All engagements are fully remote-compatible. I regularly work across IST, CET, and EST time zones. If you're in the US, UK, Europe, Southeast Asia, or the Middle East — timezone coordination is straightforward. Calls are conducted in English.

The smallest structured engagement is a Discovery & Audit — typically 2–4 weeks and focused on diagnosing your current state and architecting a roadmap. This is ideal if you want a clear picture before committing to a full build. For hands-on builds, the minimum is a 4-week Focused Build engagement. I don't take single-day consulting calls.

Data security is a first-class architectural concern — not an afterthought. For Private RAG systems: the architecture is designed to run entirely within your chosen infrastructure (your Supabase instance, your Pinecone account) with zero data transiting through any third-party service unless you explicitly choose it. For all engagements: a formal NDA is signed before any client data is shared, and access credentials are managed through environment variables — never hardcoded. Happy to discuss specific security architecture requirements on the discovery call.

Discovery & Audit: 2–4 weeks. Focused Build (single system): 4–8 weeks, including design, build, integration, and testing. The most common delivery pattern is: Week 1 — audit and architecture; Weeks 2–3 — core build sprints with weekly demos; Week 4 — integration, QA, and production deployment; Week 5 onwards — 30-day monitoring window. Timelines vary based on scope and third-party integration complexity.

Every Focused Build engagement includes a 30-day post-launch monitoring window at no additional cost — covering system health, pipeline failures, and minor adjustments. For clients who want ongoing support, optimisation, and continued development beyond that window, I offer a monthly retainer (Ongoing Partnership) that provides dedicated capacity, weekly syncs, and priority access. Details on the discovery call.
